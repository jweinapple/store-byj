About byJ.

From Baltimore to Los Angeles, byJ. is a music producer and audio engineer who treats sound like storytelling – flipping dusty samples, layering live instruments, and pushing mixes until they feel both cinematic and raw.

My sound stretches across Hip Hop, R&B, Soul, Jazz, and Rock, but it always circles back to sample-based production. I call myself a "Sound Recycler" because I love taking old songs and forgotten noises, then reshaping them into something modern and fresh. Imperfections matter – crackle, swing, subtle distortion – that's where the human side of music lives.

With 30 million+ streams worldwide, I've been blessed to work with artists like Jazz Cartier, Lil Rekk, Jayy Grams, Ben Beal, B-Jay Banks, and Sure Shot. My own series, "the first", explores love, nostalgia, and growth – drawing from real experiences of connection and memory.

Whether it's a heavy hip hop cut or a soulful ballad, I aim to create something that feels both classic and new – music that bridges eras, genres, and moods, meant to last.
